The International Radio Alphabet (NATO)

On the radio and phone, some letters are difficult to hear (P, B, V, E). The international spelling alphabet solves this problem.

E EchoF FoxtrotG GolfH Hotel
I IndiaJ JulietK KiloL Lima

Q QuebecR RomeoS SierraT Tango
